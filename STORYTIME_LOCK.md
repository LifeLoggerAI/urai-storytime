Status: LOCKED

StoryTime is intentionally frozen.

This lock exists to protect the emotional integrity of the system, the trust of families, and the long-term coherence of the experience.

## WHAT IS INCLUDED IN THE LOCK

### Child Experience
- Story structure and pacing
- Forest visuals and motion rules
- Audio behavior (voice, silence, endings)
- Night mode behavior
- Failure-mode silence

### System Behavior
- Story template loader
- Server-side voice generation + caching
- Firestore data boundaries
- No analytics, no tracking, no metrics

### Parent Experience
- Setup flow language
- Consent framing
- Absence of dashboards, reports, or summaries

## WHAT MUST NOT CHANGE
- Stories must not instruct, teach, or resolve
- No personalization based on behavior
- No rewards, streaks, or progress markers
- No questions asked of the child
- No interpretation of emotional states
- No escalation of intensity

If any of the above changes, StoryTime is no longer StoryTime.

## WHAT IS ALLOWED DURING THE LOCK
- Writing new stories (content-only)
- Fixing bugs that break silence or calm
- Infrastructure maintenance

No new concepts may be introduced.

## REVIEW WINDOW
StoryTime may only be reviewed for change after extended real-world use and only if the criteria in the Forward Criteria document are met.

Until then, the correct action is restraint.

# GOVERNANCE AMENDMENT: Unlocking Storytime V2 (Kids)

**Date:** 2024-10-27

## 1. Context

This document formally amends the project governance, specifically the freeze on `urai-storytime` as defined in `STORYTIME_LOCK.md` and `FORWARD_CRITERIA.md`.

The original lock was established to protect the integrity of a minimalist, non-performative story engine (V1). However, recent conceptual work has produced a new, distinct, and strategically vital direction: **Storytime V2 (Kids' Generational World)**.

## 2. Justification for Amendment

The criteria in `FORWARD_CRITERIA.md` were designed for a static system. They do not account for a strategic pivot to a new, parallel system. The freeze is therefore being amended not because the original criteria were met, but because the project's strategic direction has fundamentally evolved.

Storytime V2 is not an "improvement" to V1; it is a separate branch with a different purpose and architecture.

## 3. Scope of Change

- **The lock on Storytime V1 remains in place.** The original minimalist story engine is still considered complete and frozen.
- **The development of Storytime V2 (Kids' Generational World) is hereby UNLOCKED.**
- All development on V2 must adhere to the formal **"No Manipulation Children Policy"**.

## 4. New Governance

- `STORYTIME_LOCK.md` and `FORWARD_CRITERIA.md` now apply exclusively to the V1 system.
- This document serves as the official record for the un-freezing of V2 development.

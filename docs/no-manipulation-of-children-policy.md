# URAI-Storytime: No Manipulation of Children Policy

## I. Core Principle

URAI-Storytime exists to provide a calm, safe, and parent-guided storytelling experience for children (ages 3-8). Our platform is fundamentally designed to serve the well-being of the child and the peace of mind of the parent. It is not, and will never be, designed to:

- Maximize engagement through psychological manipulation.
- Capture or exploit behavioral data for commercial purposes.
- Induce emotional dependency or compulsive use.

Children are protected users, not engagement targets.

## II. Prohibited Mechanisms

To uphold our core principle, URAI-Storytime explicitly and permanently prohibits the following mechanisms:

1.  **Behavioral Profiling:** The system does not track, store, or analyze a child's in-app behaviors (e.g., tap frequency, time spent, emotional reactions) to build a profile or personalize future content dynamically. Story selection is based on age and parent choice, not on a child's inferred preferences.

2.  **Adaptive Persuasion & Reinforcement:** The platform does not use reinforcement mechanics, behavioral nudges, or escalating content loops to persuade a child to return or stay longer. Stories do not algorithmically adjust their tone, pacing, or narrative to increase retention.

3.  **Reward & Gamification Systems:** We do not use streaks, daily login bonuses, badges, XP systems, points, or competitive leaderboards. The value of the experience is in the story itself, not in an external, gamified reward.

4.  **Emotional Dependency Scripting:** The companion character is designed to be a gentle, non-verbal guide. Its script and animations are audited to ensure they do not use conditional affection, create a fear of separation, or use dependency language (e.g., "I need you," "Don't leave").

5.  **Direct Monetization Pressure on Children:** All billing and subscription management is handled exclusively through the parent dashboard. The child's experience is free of in-app purchases, upgrade prompts, premium content gates, or any other commercial messaging.

6.  **Data Exploitation:** All data related to a child is considered confidential and protected. It is never sold, shared with third-party marketers, used to train external AI models, or merged with data from any adult-facing URAI products.

## III. Data Minimization & Transparency

We adhere to a strict data minimization policy. The only child-specific data we store is their first name (or nickname) and age bracket, which is necessary for content appropriateness. Parents have the right to view, export, and permanently delete all data associated with their family at any time through the Parent Dashboard.

## IV. Enforcement & Governance

This policy is not merely a statement; it is an enforced technical and operational constraint. All new features, content, and system updates are reviewed by the URAI Foundation Oversight committee against these principles. Any proposed feature that risks violating this policy is rejected from the V1 product scope.

This document serves as a binding commitment to our users and to the public.

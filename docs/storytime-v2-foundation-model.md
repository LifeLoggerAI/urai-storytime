# Storytime V2 - Foundation-Backed Free Tier Model

This document outlines the model for a Foundation-backed free tier for Storytime V2, designed to broaden access while maintaining a sustainable, premium experience.

## Guiding Principles

- **Mission-Driven:** The free tier demonstrates URAI's commitment to ethical technology and positive childhood development.
- **Sustainable:** The model does not rely on ads, data monetization, or other extractive practices.
- **Premium Experience:** The free tier feels like a gift, not a crippled version of the paid product.

## Two-Tier Structure

### 1. Paid Family Plan (Commercial Tier)

- **Provider:** URAI Labs LLC
- **Features:**
    - Full access to all stories and content.
    - Support for multiple child profiles.
    - Parent dashboard with full controls.
    - Age progression unlocks and world expansion.
- **Monetization:** Subscription-based, potentially bundled with URAI Core family plans.

### 2. Foundation-Supported Free Access (Grant-Funded Tier)

- **Provider:** URAI Foundation
- **Features:**
    - A curated, rotating selection of core stories.
    - Single child profile.
    - Morning and Night modes only.
    - No age progression or world expansion.
- **Funding:** Sustained through grants, donations, and a portion of URAI Labs' profits.

## Rationale

This hybrid model achieves several key objectives:

- **Ethical Integrity:** Keeps the free experience clean and non-commercial.
- **Financial Sustainability:** Allows the commercial tier to fund ongoing development and innovation.
- **Grant Attractiveness:** Creates a clear, measurable impact story for grant applications ("provide X number of children with free, safe, and educational content").
- **Brand Strength:** Positions URAI as a leader in ethical AI and responsible technology.

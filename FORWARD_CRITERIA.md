StoryTime must remain unchanged unless all of the following are true:

1.  **Time Has Passed**: StoryTime has been used unchanged for months, not weeks.
2.  **Trust Is Evident Without Prompting**: Families return to StoryTime naturally, without reminders or encouragement.
3.  **No One Asks for Features**: The absence of requests is a signal of sufficiency.
4.  **Silence Is Accepted**: Endings that lead to quiet are not resisted or filled.
5.  **Boredom Feels Safe**: Repetition creates comfort, not restlessness.

Only if all five conditions are met may the system be considered for gentle expansion — and even then, only one inch at a time.

StoryTime is complete by design.

Growth is optional. Trust is not.
